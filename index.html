Simons Foundation

Simons Foundation's mission is to advance the frontiers of basic research in mathematics, physical science, and life science. This is accomplished through support of research universities and institutes, outstanding individual investigators, and large-scale, goal-driven collaborative projects. Additionally, the Simons Foundation engages in STEM education and outreach through its sponsorship of Math for America and the online publication Quanta Magazine.
